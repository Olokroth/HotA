# DREADLANDS
People across the land revere [[Entities]]—creatures from the void realm that have found their way into the continent of [[Radea]].

In the Dreadlands, horror is woven into daily life. Its inhabitants are accustomed to the unnatural strange figures lurking in the dark, eerie sounds echoing through the wilderness. Unlike the sheltered souls of the [[Three Kingdoms]], they do not recoil from the unknown but embrace it, their mannerisms subtly shaped by years of exposure. Their unsettling quirks—an unnatural calm in the presence of blood, a lingering stare, a slow, measured speech, or smiles stretched just a little too wide—make them unnerving to outsiders.

In the [[Three Kingdoms]], fear feeds superstition. Tales of possession, monstrous beasts, and corruption run rampant. The common folk whisper that contact with a Dreadlander invites sickness—or worse, transformation into something inhuman. This paranoia fuels the [[Order of the Divine Flame]], giving their crusade purpose, their cleansing justification.
