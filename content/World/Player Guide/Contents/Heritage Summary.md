# HERITAGE SUMMARY
Each heritage offers a distinct world view, evocative roles, and a rich tapestry of history that shapes every decision you make. Here’s why you might want to run one of these heritages:

## FROSTBORN
If the call of nature, ancient tradition, and survival in the face of relentless adversity stirs your soul, the Frostborn heritage offers a path like no other. Born of the harsh northern landscapes, the Frostborn hold deep reverence for their ancestors, nurturing an unbreakable bond with the land. Their story is one of endurance, honour, and the perpetual struggle against both the elements and the encroachments of modernity.

### Why choose Frostborn?
- **Primal Connection:** Become a hunter, tracker, or spiritual leader with deep-rooted ties to the natural world.
- **Tradition and Honour:** Live out a narrative where ancient customs and the honour of your kin serve as guiding pillars in a challenging environment.
- **Survival Against Tough Odds:** Whether leading your people or mastering the wilderness as a survivalist, experience the raw beauty and brutality of life in the North.

This heritage is perfect for players who crave a journey of rugged exploration, spiritual introspection, and enduring resilience.

## IRON EMPIRE
Imagine living in a realm where the very air is charged with rebellion, oppression, and a legacy of conquest. In the Iron Empire, every corner of the land is a reminder of a past when independent nations fell under the weight of an all-powerful regime. Whether you revel in the role of a dissenting rebel, a steadfast defender of an oppressive monarchy, or even an elite who challenges the status quo, your character will navigate a world where high stakes, political intrigue, and personal sacrifice are the norm.

### Why choose the Iron Empire?
- **Dynamic Conflict:** Engage in narratives fueled by the clash between oppressive authorities and the masses yearning for freedom.
- **Deep Class Tensions:** Decide whether your character stands with the downtrodden labourers or thrives as part of the noble elite.
- **Rich Historical Echoes:** Experience the weight of ancient conquests and the remnants of lost cultures that continue to haunt the present.

Whether you opt for the life of a knight, soldier, cleric, or even a humble miner or pilgrim, the Iron Empire promises a world where every action is underscored by historical scars and ambitious momentum.

## TU'NAUT
Step into a society defined by the brilliance of its diverse cultures and the power of intellectual audacity. The Tu’Naut heritage is a celebration of unity in diversity—where coastal cities are hubs of trade, invention, and secretive power plays. Here, the strength of a character isn’t measured by physical might but by the sharpness of the mind, the daring of their inventions, and the subtle influence exerted from behind the scenes.

### Why choose Tu’Naut?
- **Intellectual Prowess:** Play as an engineer, philosopher, scientist, or physician, where every discovery and innovation can tip the balance of power.
- **Cultural Fusion:** Embrace a heritage where differences are celebrated, offering diverse perspectives and intriguing backstories.
- **Undercover Intrigue:** Whether as a shipwright guiding maritime expeditions or a diplomat plotting power moves, delve into narratives full of covert maneuvers and quiet revolutions.  

This heritage is ideal if you see your character as a problem-solver, an inventor, or a mastermind whose intellect shapes the future in unexpected ways.

## IN SUMMARY
Each heritage is a portal into a world teeming with conflict, innovation, or timeless tradition:
- **Iron Empire:** For dramatic power struggles, political scheming, and narratives of rebellion or loyalty.
- **Tu’Naut:** For cerebral adventures, inventive breakthroughs, and the thrill of a culturally rich society.
- **Frostborn:** For a soulful connection to nature, a life of tradition and honour, and the grit required to survive harsh landscapes.  

Ultimately, your choice determines not just your role but the very lens through which you experience the game’s world. These heritages are more than just profiles—they are invitations to step into legends of rebellion, ingenuity, or survival. Which legacy will begin your destiny?
