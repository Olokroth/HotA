#### Night of the Hollow Veil
**Day:** 225
**Calendar:** 15th Withering, Crest
**Tags:** <mark style="color: yellow"></mark>

Held during the Winter Solstice, this solemn tradition marks the longest night in the [[Dreadlands - Narrator#DREADLANDS|Dreadlands]]—a rare stretch of darkness when [[Spirit]] are most abundant across the land. The [[Dreadlands - Narrator#DREADLANDS|Forsaken]] roam silently under shadow and moonlight, not to wait for [[Spirit|Spirits]] to come—but to seek them out. Like a perilous and sacred hunt, they scour desolate woods, ruined shrines, and forgotten graves, each hoping to discover a [[Spirit|Spirit]] willing to possess them. Such communion may grant cryptic prophecy, ancestral insight, or forbidden truths—but always at great risk. Not all [[Spirit|Spirits]] are kind. Not all possessions are brief. And not all who hunt return.
