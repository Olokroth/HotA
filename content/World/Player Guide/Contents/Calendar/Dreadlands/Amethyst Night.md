#### Amethyst Night
**Calendar:** Special, cycles every 100 years
**Tags:** <mark style="color: yellow"></mark>

An astronomical event in which the night sky turns a deep purple and stars shine brighter than before; new constellations appear in the sky. On this night, [[Tear, Description]] scars are visible to the naked eye. Fearful residents stay in their homes, and guards are out in full force. This is the most celebrated event amongst [[Dreadlands - Narrator#DREADLANDS|Forsaken]].
