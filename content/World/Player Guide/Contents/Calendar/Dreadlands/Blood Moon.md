#### Blood Moon
**Calendar:** Special, lasts a week

In the [[Dreadlands#DREADLANDS|Dreadlands]], the blood moon marks a sacred time when worshippers honour the region’s ruling [[Dreadlands#Entities|Entities]] through ritual sacrifice. Animal offerings are common, though some sects turn to human sacrifice in pursuit of divine favour.
