#### Endalaus Sól (Summer Solstice)
**Day:** 90
**Calendar:** 30th Blooming, Wane
**Tags:** <mark style="color: yellow"></mark>

[[Phonetic Dictionary#**Endalaus Sól (en·da·loys sohl)**|Pronunciation]]: A radiant [[Jörden]] celebration of summer’s endless light, held during the season of the midnight sun. With night nearly vanished, the people revel in warmth, vitality, and the ever-shining sky.
