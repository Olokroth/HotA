#### Glæsilegra Ljósa (Winter Solstice)
**Day:** 225
**Calendar:** 15th Withering, Crest
**Tags:** <mark style="color: yellow"></mark>

[[Phonetic Dictionary#**Glæsilegra Ljósa (gly·sih·leh·grah lyo·sah)**|Pronunciation]]: In the rugged lands of [[Jörden]], the Winter Solstice marks the turning of the sun—a moment when light begins its slow return. Tribes gather beneath stars around towering bonfires, casting light into the longest night. Dancers circle the blaze, songs echo in the chill air, and elders tell tales of perseverance and rebirth. Lights often appear overhead in the night's sky, illuminating the land.
