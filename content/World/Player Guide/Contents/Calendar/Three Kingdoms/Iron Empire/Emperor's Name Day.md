#### Emperor's Name Day
**Day:** 101 (Special)
**Calendar:** 11th Reaping, Wax
**Tags:** <mark style="color: yellow"></mark>

A national celebration of the reigning emperor’s birthday, marked by fire rituals, parades, and renewed vows of loyalty throughout the Iron Empire. The current [[Emperor Calian Lorringer]]'s was born on the 11th of the Reaping, Wax.
