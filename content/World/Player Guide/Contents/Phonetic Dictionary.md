### **Radea (ray·dee·ah)**
Name of the continent comprised of the Dreadlands, Jörden, Iron Empire and Tu’Naut

### Oceans
#### **Havinne Ocean (ha·vine)**
An ocean to the west of Radea
#### **Thalwyn Sea (thal·win)**
An ocean to the north and east of Radea
#### **Zaelrin Ocean (zal·rin)**
An ocean to the south of Radea
