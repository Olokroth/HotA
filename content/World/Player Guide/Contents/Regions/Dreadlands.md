# DREADLANDS
People across the land revere Entities—creatures from the void realm that have found their way into the continent of Radea.

In the Dreadlands, horror is woven into daily life. Its inhabitants are accustomed to the unnatural strange figures lurking in the dark, eerie sounds echoing through the wilderness. Unlike the sheltered souls of the Three Kingdoms, they do not recoil from the unknown but embrace it, their mannerisms subtly shaped by years of exposure. Their unsettling quirks—an unnatural calm in the presence of blood, a lingering stare, a slow, measured speech, or smiles stretched just a little too wide—make them unnerving to outsiders.

In the Three Kingdoms, fear feeds superstition. Tales of possession, monstrous beasts, and corruption run rampant. The common folk whisper that contact with a Dreadlander invites sickness—or worse, transformation into something inhuman. This paranoia fuels the Order of the Divine Flame, giving their crusade purpose, their cleansing justification.

## Void
A realm of shifting unreality—black, writhing, and endless. From its depths, monsters emerged, tainting the land and corrupting the very fabric of the land.

## Corruption
It is said that the very earth of the Dreadlands twists those who linger within too long, warping mind and body alike. The corruption is especially ruthless toward those whose hearts are impure, seeping into their souls until they are no longer the same.

## Null Stone

> ![[null-stone.webp]]

A jet-black crystal that seems to devour light at its core, shrouded in an unnatural darkness. Incredibly strong yet deceptively light, its edges are lethally sharp. Feared for its potential for corruption, it is outlawed as contraband within the Three Kingdoms.

## Void-Touched
Legends speak of arcane sorcerers who bartered their very souls for power, forging pacts with the eldritch horrors of the Void. In exchange, they gained mastery over its corrupt energies, bending reality to their will—but at a cost beyond mortal comprehension.

## Ravenous
Whispers tell of those who have succumbed to the Dreadlands' corruption, their minds eroded until only hunger remains. No longer human, they roam with insatiable craving, seeking flesh to sate their endless appetite.

## Entities
Beings of the Void, these eldritch beings defy natural law, slipping into the world from realms unknown. Their presence warps reality, twisting minds and corrupting all they touch.

## Mutation
[[#Corruption]] warps the body and soul of those affected.
