# TU'NAUT

![[tuNaut.webp]]

A nation of innovators and seafarers, the Southerners reject the constraints of religion, believing true enlightenment lies in the boundless exploration of the mind. Governed by a parliament of intellectuals, each member nation holds a seat, ensuring a balance of wisdom and progress. Justice is not dictated by rulers but upheld through fair trials, where criminals stand before their peers and receive proper legal representation.

Violence is scorned, for the people of Tu’Naut hold reason and intellect above brute force—proof that strength need not be measured in bloodshed.

## CULTURE
Tu’Naut is a haven for free thinkers—bold individuals with sharp minds and strong convictions. A nation steeped in knowledge and philosophy, it thrives on intellectual discourse and the pursuit of progress.

Its diverse cultures are bound not by uniformity, but by mutual respect, celebrating differences as the foundation of their unity.

Along its coasts, skilled shipwrights and seasoned sailors drive the nation’s maritime industry, mastering transport and fishing with precision and innovation.

## EDUCATION
The government-established schools are free and open to all, covering education from early learning to advanced adult studies. Supervised public records are displayed, ensuring transparency. Every individual raised in Tu’Naut is taught to write fluently in both their native tongue and the Common language.

The exclusive school [[House of Velinsta|Velinsta]], in the capital of [[Kata]] is well renowned for its education in the art of carnal pleasures. Those who graduate from the school are known as Vels and Vens—titles denoting females and males, respectively. Renowned for their beauty, grace, and intellect, they are widely admired and frequently sought after—even as desirable prospects for marriage. All business is overseen directly by the [[House of Velinsta]], guided by its matrons and patrons. The house operates as a political force, weaving intricate schemes to bolster the nation’s ambitions.

## ENTERTAINMENT
A culture rich in strategy and artistry. Grand tournaments showcase mastery of complex tactical games, while musical performances, intricate board games, and fine art provide both recreation and refinement. Reading, too, is highly valued, serving as a gateway to knowledge and imagination.

## FOOD
Their cuisine thrives on bold flavours and fresh ingredients—exotic spices and fragrant herbs elevate every dish, while bountiful seafood, rice, vibrant fruits, and lush vegetables sustain their people.

## RELATIONS
They uphold the philosophy of mutual growth, forging alliances built on shared knowledge and prosperity.

### Frostborn
An invaluable trade partner, exchanging advanced technologies and intellectual pursuits for the rare minerals for technologies.

### Iron Empire
Though vastly different in values, they recognize the Iron Empire’s industrial prowess. On occasion, they commission their factories to manufacture innovative designs, securing access to new engineering marvels.

## REGION
A land of warmth and contrast—sun-drenched deserts and glimmering oases offer sanctuary, while sprawling isles dot the coastline. With effortless access to the sea, their dominion thrives on exploration, trade, and maritime strength.

## RELIGION
Faith is scarce, a quiet presence rather than a guiding force. Those who practice tend to have ties to foreign lands, hail from border towns, or have glimpsed the horrors beyond the wall and survived—changed by what they saw.

## SOCIETY
The birth rate is moderately lower than in the [[Iron Empire]], as priorities differ. While many pursue education with dedication, others see it as secondary to trade, craftsmanship, or survival.

## TECHNOLOGY
Innovation flourishes, driven by both necessity and ambition. Warfare evolves with the volatile force of explosive black powder and newly designed single-shot projectile weapons. Precision lenses sharpen sight for navigation and study, while advanced shipbuilding expands the frontiers of maritime exploration—yet breakthroughs continue to emerge.

The latest technological breakthrough is underway in [[Kata]], where scientists have devised a method to harness the kinetic energy of running water, storing it within concealed metal containers for later use. Similarly, sunlight can now be captured and preserved, powering bulbs that serve as steady torches during the dark hours.
