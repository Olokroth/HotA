**Calendar:** No knowledge

An astronomical event in which the night sky turns a deep purple and stars shine brighter than before; new constellations appear in the sky. On this night, [[Tear]] scars are visible to the naked eye. Fearful residents stay in their homes, and guards are out in full force.
