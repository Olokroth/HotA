A brutal chapter in the empire’s history, sparked by [[Divine Archpriest Benjamin Kane]] and his fanatical [[Order of the Divine Flame]]. Exploiting fears of foreign corruption, [[Divine Archpriest Benjamin Kane|Kane]] rallied the masses to purge those labelled impure—whether [[Mutation|mutated]], deformed, or politically outcast. Victims were hunted, caged, and burned alive in public spectacle, their deaths fuelling a firestorm of zeal that consumed the empire in holy terror.
