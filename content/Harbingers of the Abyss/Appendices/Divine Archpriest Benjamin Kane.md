**THE FLAME’S IGNITING VOICE**
Benjamin Kane was the prophet of fire — a man who turned fear into faith and faith into power. In the wake of the [[Void-Touched|altered]]’s discovery, he incited the [[Great Purification]], orchestrating mass executions that seared the [[Order of the Divine Flame]] into the soul of the Empire. As [[Emperor Rhoq the First|Rhoq]]’s spiritual ally and later [[Emperor Calian Lorringer|Calian]]’s mentor, Kane ensured the [[Order of the Divine Flame|Order]]’s rise from fringe sect to state religion. He died revered by the faithful and feared by all others — a martyr to some, a monster to many.

His sermons lit the pyres, and his doctrine sanctified cruelty. Though long dead, his legacy burns on in every [[Flamebearer|Flamebearer]]’s torch.
