An enduring pact forged in desperation between the realms of [[Jörden]], the [[Iron Empire]], and [[Tu'Naut]]—a coalition born not of trust, but of shared survival. Faced with the rising tide of the [[Void]], a supernatural plague threatening the extinction of humankind, the Sovereign Concord bound old enemies in uneasy unity. Beneath the surface of alliance, ancestral grudges and competing ambitions fester, threatening the fragile balance. The treaty was solemnized by [[Emperor Rhoq the First]], representing the [[Iron Empire]], alongside the tribal chieftains of [[Jörden]] and the ruling council of [[Tu'Naut]].
