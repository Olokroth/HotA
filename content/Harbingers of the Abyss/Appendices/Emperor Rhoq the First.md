**THE IRON CONQUEROR**
Rhoq the First rose from the chaos of fractured nations, forging the [[Iron Empire]] through relentless conquest and brutal discipline. A master of war and will, he elevated the [[Order of the Divine Flame]] from obscurity to state religion after endorsing the [[Great Purification]], aligning its doctrine of cleansing with his vision of unity through domination. Though he signed [[The Sovereign Concord]] of the [[Three Kingdoms]], it was a pause, not peace. He died as he ruled — unyielding, entombed beneath [[Eseryn]] in black iron.

*"Let our might be felt across the land, like thunder rolling from the mountains. Let our glory be swift and terrible, a storm that leaves no shelter. Let our enemies be trampled beneath our feet, their names forgotten in the dust.*

*Strength from iron — and from iron, we stand. We are the hammer. We are the flame. We are the will unbroken.*

*So speaks the Iron Emperor. So marches the Empire Eternal."*
*- Rhoq Lorringer*
