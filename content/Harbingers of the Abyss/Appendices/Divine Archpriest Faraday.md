**THE FLAME INCARNATE**
Faraday inherited the [[Order of the Divine Flame|Order]] not as a servant of its ideals, but as a master of its machinery. Where [[Divine Archpriest Benjamin Kane|Kane]] inspired, Faraday controls. As Divine Archpriest, he has transformed the [[Order of the Divine Flame|Order]] into a theocratic engine of surveillance, fear, and obedience. He rules the [[Iron Empire|Empire]] in all but name, his influence stretching from the pulpits to the palace. To the faithful, he is the voice of divine judgement. To the rest, he is the architect of tyranny — and the true power behind the Iron Throne.
