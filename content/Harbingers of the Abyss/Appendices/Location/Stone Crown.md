[[Phonetic Dictionary#**buren (bur·en) klauk (klaa·uhk)**|Pronunciation]]
Spacious flat fields stretch outward, embracing a ring of towering stone pillars that rise from the earth like the sculpted crown of nature itself—a solemn monument to the land’s enduring strength and beauty.

It is the sacred gathering place of the tribes, where celebrations, councils, and great meetings unite the people.
