[[Phonetic Dictionary#**Kata (kah·tah)**|Pronunciation]]
The city thrives on kinetic electricity, harnessed from water turbines, alongside the scorching heat of the sun, converted into power for its infrastructure and emerging technologies. As night falls, its illuminated skyline gleams like a beacon against the darkness.

Most homes are built from sturdy sandstone bricks, bound with mortar, their thatched roofs offering practical insulation. In contrast, the affluent dwellings display grandeur—crafted from pristine marble, their rooftops fashioned from polished wood or marble itself, standing as symbols of wealth and refinement.
