[[Phonetic Dictionary#**Eseryn (ess·ee·rin)**|Pronunciation]]
A sprawling metropolis, divided into distinct districts. At its heart, a grand castle rises atop a fortified hill, encircled by formidable stone walls that shield the Emperor’s stronghold and the lavish estates of the nobility. A second wall stretches across the perimeter, safeguarding the common districts beyond.

The industrial sector, a labyrinth of factories and workshops, hums with constant activity. Machines roar, workers toil, and the air is thick with the scent of coal and metal. This district fuels the empire’s ambitions, forging weapons, machinery, and goods that shape its future.

Beyond lies the common quarter, a stark contrast to the nobility’s grandeur. Brick and stone homes offer stability to the fortunate, while wooden dwellings and the shantytown’s ramshackle hovels bear the weight of hardship. The city thrives, its divisions clear but its pulse unwavering.

The heart of the empire, [[Phonetic Dictionary#**Eseryn (ess·ee·rin)**|Eseryn]] stands as both its capital and the stronghold of the [[Order of the Divine Flame]]. It is the seat of power, where faith and authority intertwine.
