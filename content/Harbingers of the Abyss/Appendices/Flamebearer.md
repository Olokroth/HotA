Devoted warriors of the Order of the Divine Flame, sworn to seek out and cleanse corruption with righteous fire. They lead missionary groups, travelling across the [[content/World/Contents/Three Kingdoms]] and venturing into the [[Dreadlands]] on perilous pilgrimages, burning away impurity wherever they find it.
