**Day:** 43
**Calendar:** 13th Blooming, Crest

Observed in the heart of the Iron Empire, this solemn and fervent holiday marks the anniversary of the [[Great Purification]]. The capital erupts in ritual fire as citizens gather to cast writings, effigies, and symbols of dissent into towering pyres. Guided by the fanatical [[Order of the Divine Flame]], the celebration opens with the execution of the condemned—if any remain—whose public immolation reaffirms the empire’s devotion to purity. For many, it's a day of righteous zeal; for others, a grim reminder of a legacy forged in fire and fear.
