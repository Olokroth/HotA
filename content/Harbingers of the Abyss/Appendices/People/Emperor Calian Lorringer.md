**THE HOLLOW CROWN**
Raised in the shadow of a legend, Calian Lorringer grew up under the watchful eye of [[Divine Archpriest Benjamin Kane|Benjamin Kane]], who served as his advisor and spiritual guide after [[Emperor Rhoq the First|Rhoq]]’s death. Meek and impressionable, Calian absorbed the [[Order of the Divine Flame|Order]]’s doctrine without question, shaped more by fear than conviction. Though he ascended the throne with the people’s hope, he ruled with hesitation, deferring to the clergy at every turn. Only in recent years has illness overtaken him, leaving him frail and withdrawn. Now, the Empire moves without him — and the [[Order of the Divine Flame|Flame]] burns unchecked.
