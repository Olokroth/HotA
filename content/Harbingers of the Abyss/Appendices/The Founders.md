The Founders of [[Tu'Naut|Tu'Naut]] were the first generation to set foot on [[Radea]], having cast off the shackles of enslavement in a distant land. More than pioneers, they were visionaries and warriors of spirit—resilient, defiant, and united by the dream of sovereignty. Their arrival marked the beginning of a free nation, and their legacy endures in the songs, stories, and sacred rites of the [[Tu'Naut|Tu'Naut]] people.
