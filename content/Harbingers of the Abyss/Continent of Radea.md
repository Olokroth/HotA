# RADEA

![[map-radea.webp]]

## HISTORY
A century ago (13 BFE), the continent of [[Radea]] was a mosaic of independent nations. Seeking absolute power, the war-hungry nation of [[Phonetic Dictionary#**Ferrum (fur·uhm)**|Ferrum]] ignited a brutal campaign of conquest, forging the formidable [[Iron Empire]] from the blood of its rivals. Refugees fled north, taking shelter among the resilient [[Jörden|Northern Tribes]], their harsh homeland a natural fortress against invasion. In defiance, several nations banded together, founding [[Tu'Naut]] (11 BFE) to resist [[Iron Empire|Imperial expansion]]. Others, desperate to escape the carnage, ventured into the western wastelands. There, they encountered a cosmic being and established **Shatterfall** at the site of an ancient meteor, made up of [[Null Stone|black stone]] (6 BFE).

For twelve long years, war ravaged the land. But as the conflict endured, something unnatural began to seep from the wastelands. [[Corruption]] took root—twisting people into monstrous forms, unleashing horrors never before seen, and awakening arcane forces. The [[Iron Empire]] soon faced an affliction, an insidious plague spreading through its cities. In response, the [[Order of the Divine Flame]] was founded to combat the growing sickness. What followed was the [[Great Purification]] (1 BFE)—a fanatical purge that consumed the empire’s focus.

With new threats rising and old grudges fading before an unknown enemy, war came to an abrupt halt. The three surviving nations—[[Tu'Naut]], the [[Jörden|Northern Tribes]], and the [[Iron Empire]]—brokered a fragile peace, forging the [[Three Kingdoms]] (1 FE). A treaty defined their borders, marking a vast and uninhabitable "no man's land" between their territories. Trade routes were established, and a unified [[Calendar|calendar]] set the course for a new era. Yet looming above all was the dire need for defense. To shield their lands from the corruption spilling from the [[Dreadlands]], construction of a colossal wall began—a barrier that would take 36 years to complete (36 FE).

Now, in the present day (87 FE), the [[Great Wall|great walls]] still hold, standing as humanity’s last safeguard against the darkness beyond. But the [[Corruption|corruption]] festers, growing stronger with each passing year. The [[Order of the Divine Flame]] continues its solemn crusade, sending pilgrims led by [[Flamebearer|Flamebearers]] into the [[Dreadlands]], battling a supernatural force that refuses to be contained.
