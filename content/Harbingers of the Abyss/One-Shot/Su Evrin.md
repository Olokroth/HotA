Once a devout Flamebearer from Eseryn, they now roam the land, their faith scorched by the screams of their brother as he was burned alive by the Divine Order. The pyre that consumed him also ignited their reckoning. Wrestling with the ruins of their beliefs, they carry his greatsword not just as a tribute, but as a bloodbound vow. Wherever the Order casts its shadow, they bring light to the oppressed—offering shelter, justice, and the edge of their blade. But beneath every act of mercy simmers a singular goal: to one day find the priest who lit the flame, and split him apart like the Order split their world.
