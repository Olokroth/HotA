While on an expedition to gather rare minerals for a critical experiment, a sudden rockslide claimed their spouse’s life. Grief-stricken but undeterred, they returned to town and hired a guide—determined to venture into the Dreadlands, a place whispered to defy reality itself. There, they hoped to find the impossible: a cure for death.
