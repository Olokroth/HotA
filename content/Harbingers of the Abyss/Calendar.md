# CALENDAR
There are...
9 months in a year, 30 days in a month, and 24 hours in a day, totalling 270 days in a year.

## DAYS OF THE WEEK
Each day is a reference to a flame growing in intensity. It may be referred to by its name or followed by “day” (e.g., Lustre, Lustreday or Lustre’s day).
- *KINDLE – Start of the week*
- *FLICKER*
- *GLINT*
- *LUSTRE – Height of the week*
- *SHIMMER*
- *BRILL*
- *DOUSE – End of the week*

## SEASONS
There are three seasons: Bloom(ing), Reap(ing), and Wither(ing). Each season is 90 days and can be divided into three 30-day months/phases: Wax, Crest and Wane (e.g. Crest phase of the Bloom or Blooming season).

**BLOOM(ING): Season of Regrowth**
- *Blooming, Wax (Budding)*
- *Blooming, Crest (Unfolding)*
- *Blooming, Wane (Maturity & Pollination)*

**REAP(ING): Season of Harvest**
- *Reaping, Wax (Gathering)*
- *Reaping, Crest (Threshing)*
- *Reaping, Wane (Storing)*

**WITHER(ING): Season of Decay**
- *Withering, Wax (Wilting & Decay)*
- *Withering, Crest (Withering & Breakdown)*
- *Withering, Wane (Return to the Earth)*
