*The dagger fell as crimson ichor welled up along the division of lacerated flesh on his right palm. He gasped at the suddenness of the injury. Cupping his hand, blood pooled before running over and down the length of his forearm, dripping onto the sodden ash below. His left hand gripped the black stone pendant around his neck, and with a soft exhale, he cleared his mind to vividly picture the next vital moments.*

*Squeezing the stone firmly, a whispered plea to a forsaken god escaped his parched lips, an accord struck. As if gravity forgot itself, the sanguineous fluid floated upwards above the laceration to converge into a flawless sphere. Time slowed as he let in a deep breath, counting each of life's pulses. At the ninth beat, liquid tendrils shot out from the orb and plunged straight into the heart of the foul beast charging forward. With a trembling crash, its body kicked up plumes of billowing dust around itself as if in defiance of its demise. Releasing a sigh of relief, he let go of his worries in the stillness of the moment. The price, no matter the cost, was worth it.*
